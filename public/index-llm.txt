2025 台灣綜合所得稅計算器
網址：https://tsesc.github.io/tw-tax-calculator-ex/

功能摘要：
本計算器依據台灣 2025 年最新稅法，可即時計算單身或已婚（三種方式）應納稅額與有效稅率；顯示幼兒學前、長期照顧、教育學費、房屋租金等各項扣除額明細，並自動比較最省稅方案，提供節稅建議。

English Summary:
This calculator follows Taiwan's 2025 Individual Income Tax rules. Enter salary and deduction information to instantly obtain payable tax, effective rate, and the most tax-efficient filing option (combined, salary-separate, or all-separate for married couples). It details deductions such as preschool children, long-term care, tuition, rental expense, and more.
